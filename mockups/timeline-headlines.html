Timeline Headlines — Mockups

Three touch-friendly approaches. All use real data, no hover dependencies.

A: Vertical List B: Scrolling Cards C: Tap-to-Expand

A: Vertical List

Replaces the dot timeline with a vertical list. Every headline is always visible. Collapsed by default showing the 3 most recent; tap to expand. Each row is a 44px+ tap target. Most readable on narrow screens.

    B: Scrolling Cards

    Horizontal swipe strip. Each sample is a card with date + headline. Native mobile scroll with snap points. Replaces dots entirely. Most headlines visible without interaction, scroll for the rest.

    C: Tap-to-Expand

    Keeps the compact dot layout with 28px tap targets (vs 10px originals). Tapping a dot reveals the headline in a panel below. Tapping another dot switches; same dot closes. Compact by default, progressive disclosure. Dots still show status color and date range.
